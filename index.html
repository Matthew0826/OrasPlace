Ora's Place Inc.

Putting lives and families back together as one.

Ora's Place, Inc. is committed to respecting the rights of others, uplifting temperament, rebuilding self-esteem, and empowerment, and putting lives, and families back together as one!

Ora's Place, Inc. is a place where families, can enjoy the presence of temporary housing with their children while seeking, and awaiting permanent housing.
Ora’s Place, Inc. is a non-profit organization with 501 (c) (3) status assisting families and individuals who are experiencing homelessness with supportive services.

These families and individuals may or may not exceed The Department of Housing and Community Development (DHCD) emergency assistance (ea) eligibility due to being over the federal poverty guidelines, of 200%.

Ora’s Place, Inc. believes that no family should have to quit their employment to become eligible for temporary shelter, and/or be left out without housing.
No family will be left behind!
